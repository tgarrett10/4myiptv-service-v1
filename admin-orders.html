Order Workflow

Use this flow to move each customer cleanly from intake to completion.

New Requests

Review new form submissions and confirm the customer details before taking action.

  • Check name, contact details, and requested service type
  • Review device information or online-only needs
  • Confirm the next step before messaging the customer

Awaiting Contact

Send the first response, answer questions, and guide the customer toward the best next step.

  • Choose the correct quote or follow-up template
  • Clarify device, service term, or install method if needed
  • Move the order to the next stage after contact
