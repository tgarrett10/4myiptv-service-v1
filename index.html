A Simpler Path From Request to Setup

This service works best when customers know what to expect up front. The request and support path is built to feel clear and organized from the beginning.

Service-Only Orders

Customers requesting service without a device typically receive setup details within 24 hours after the request is reviewed and approved.

Shipped Device Orders

Prepared device orders typically ship within 24 to 48 hours, and a FedEx tracking number is provided after shipment.

Support When You Need It

Existing customers can come back for renewal help, troubleshooting, setup guidance, and app update support.
